Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

A complete toolkit for managing the full lifecycle of college clubs and events.

Event Management

Create, update, and manage events with custom registration form fields, deadlines, and participant limits.

Team Invitations

Students invite teammates after registering. Accept, reject, or re-invite — with full status visibility at all times.

Role-based Access

Separate dashboards and permissions for colleges, clubs, and students. Everyone sees exactly what they need.

Event Visibility Modes

Set events as College Exclusive or Open to All — letting students from other colleges discover and register too.

OTP Verification

Secure signup with OTP sent via Gmail. No fake accounts, no clutter — just verified users.

Participant Tracking

Real-time tabular view of all registered students per event. Export-ready and always up to date.

How it works

Up and running in minutes

No complex setup. Just register, create, and go.

1

College registers

A college admin signs up, verifies via OTP, and sets up the college profile.

2

Clubs are created

Club admins register under the college and set up their club page with coordinators and social links.

3

Events go live

Clubs create events with custom forms, deadlines, and visibility settings.

4

Students participate

Students register, form teams, and track everything from their dashboard.
